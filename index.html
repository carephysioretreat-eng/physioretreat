My speciality

Back, Neck & Shoulder — done right

Most pain I treat starts in these three areas. Here's how focused, one-on-one care gets you moving freely again.

Back & Spine

Slip disc, sciatica, lower-back stiffness and posture-related pain.

  • Manual therapy & mobilisation
  • Core & spine strengthening
  • Ergonomic correction

Neck & Cervical

Tech-neck, cervical spondylosis, stiffness and posture headaches.

  • Release & traction techniques
  • Posture & mobility drills
  • Headache relief routines

Shoulder

Frozen shoulder, rotator cuff, impingement and limited movement.

  • Range-of-motion recovery
  • Strength & stability work
  • Pain-free daily function
